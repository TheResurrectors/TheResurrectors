Our Project

Our topic for our FLL project is Deadzones.
“Dead Zones” are water bodies which are
oxygen-deprived. They are caused by fertilizer runoff into water bodies.
We are working to solve these Dead Zones to improve tourism, economics
and the environments of the regions they affect.
